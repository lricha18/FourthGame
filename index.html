Capture the Star

  • High Concept: Be the first player to accumulate 20 points either by capturing the star or shooting the other player.
  • Player Experience Goals: Some experience with capture the flag and shooting games might be needed.
  • Genre: This game is a two person arcade game.
  • Game play: This game supports two players on a single keyboard. Player 1 flies their player with the arrow keys and fires bullets with the M key. Player 2 flies around using the combination of the WASD keys corresponding to up, left, down, and right respectively and fires bullets with the F key. This game detects collisions between the two players and resets them to the starting positions. The game also detects a player colliding with the star which will increase their score and create a new star at a random location on the screen. Lastly, this game detects a bullet hitting a player. When this happens, the shooter's score increases, and the player hit is reset to the starting position.
  • Prototype goal: This prototype is to create a game with the capture objective. Also, this game was made to learn how to fire projectiles at other players and detect collisions.
  • Features: 2 Players, each can fly a UFO around and shoot at each other while capturing a star.
  • Setting/Premise: This game takes place at the edge of the Andromeda galaxy.
  • Story: The spacecrafts are out hunting stars to collect when they encounter each other, so they fight to collect the most stars.
  • Target Audience: This game targets an audience of children and adults of all ages.
  • Play time: 2-5 Minutes. Can be replayed multiple times.
  • Strategies: The best strategy is not to fly straight toward the stars. A player must take into account the position of the other player, where their bullets are, and where the star is, if they plan to win this game.
