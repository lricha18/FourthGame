Learn to count to 10!

  • High Concept: Control the space ship to collect the numbers in order.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players do not necessarily need to know how to count to ten but some knowledge of what numbers are would help.
  • Genre: This is a children's learning game.
  • Game play: This game supports one player who my fly around the screen searching for the numbers 1-10. The game is able to detect if the player is picking up the numbers in order. If a number is picked up in order, a yay sound will play, if not a buzzer sound will play. When a player finishes, they are congragulated on their counting skills and are asked if they would like to play again. If they click the mouse the game resets.
  • Prototype goal: This prototype is to create a simple working version of a game where a player may collect things in a certain order. Numbers can be substituted for letters at a later time.
  • Features: Game a finite state machine that detects if a player has collected the numbers in the correct order or not. The game may also be replayed an infinite amount of times because the numbers are randomly placed throughout the world.
  • Setting/Premise: This game takes place in a grassy field in the state of Oregon
  • Story: The spacecraft is trying to figure out how the strange symbols around the world and with the help of the player they learn how to count together.
  • Target Audience: This game targets an audience of children ages 4 and under.
  • Play time: 1-5 Minutes depending on counting skills. Can be replayed multiple times.
  • Strategies: The only real strategy other than collecting the numbers in order is remembering where each number is as you fly by it.
